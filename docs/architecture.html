Routing
auto_decline_bureauP1 Wilful defaulter · NPA · Bureau hard stop flags → immediate decline, no human review
auto_decline_policyP2 DSCR < 1.0 · Policy hard stop → auto_decline flag set by rules engine
escalateP3 ≥ 2 HIGH contradictions → senior credit committee queue
fast_track_approveP4 All agents LOW · no conditions · no hard stops → approved without human step

↓ underwriter_decision (or auto-decision) passed to compile_report
