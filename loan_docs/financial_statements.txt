NOTE: Revenue from Operations above (₹3,94,82,000 / ₹394.82 lakh)
differs from the ₹421 lakh stated in the loan application.
The loan application figure appears to include GST collected
(~₹26 lakh) within the revenue number, which is non-standard.
Underwriters should use the audited figure of ₹394.82 lakh
as the reliable revenue base.

EBITDA NOTE: EBITDA margin has declined from 17.4% to 14.6%
due to cotton price inflation of ~18% YoY. This is more severe
than the 15.0% stated in the loan application, which appears
to have used a slightly different cost allocation methodology.
